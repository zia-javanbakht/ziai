ziPomo

A minimalistic Pomodoro timer for macOS that lives in your menu bar. Stay focused, take breaks, and get things done.

focus without interruption

ziPomo sits quietly in your menu bar, counting down your work sessions so you can concentrate on what matters. No distracting windows, no clutter — just a simple timer that keeps you on track. Puase/Restart any time using CMD+Left-click.

breaks on your terms

Breaks are suggested, never enforced. When a break comes up, you decide whether to take it or skip it (with a simple click) and keep working. ziPomo adapts to your rhythm, not the other way around.

quick menu access

Pause, advance, or reset your timer with a single click. Switch between profiles and access settings instantly — everything you need is just a menu bar click away.

simple adjustable cycles

Customise work and break durations to suit your workflow. Create multiple profiles for different tasks, toggle auto-start behaviour, and fine-tune your Pomodoro experience with straightforward settings.
